ONE TO WATCH

The Speedmaster 38 mm

22 MARCH 2017

The Speedmaster 38 mm stays true to the spirit of the original, but takes a fresh new direction.

What makes the Speedmaster 38 mm so special? Let's start with the bicolour dial. Its luscious colour gives the watch its “Cappuccino” nickname and its soft, inviting tone is complemented by three oval subdials in brown.

And how's this for clever? OMEGA has been able to add a diamond-paved bezel while also keeping the Speedmaster’s famous tachymeter scale. This dual bezel design has enabled the brand to add the beauty of diamonds and also retain the sporty DNA of the original Speedmaster.

It looks just as good on the reverse side too. The stainless steel and 18K Sedna™ gold case is stamped with OMEGA’s iconic Seahorse medallion, inspired by the canal boats of Venice.

The taupe-brown leather strap is the ultimate finishing touch; and if you like the look of the “Cappuccino”, well, we're only getting started. The complete collection will include 14 models, all with varying colours and distinct touches.
